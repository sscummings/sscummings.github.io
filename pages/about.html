Hello! I'm Stephen. I make things.

Stephen Cummings is a photographer, designer, editor, and artist, currently based in Brooklyn, New York. He studied painting and drawing at Arizona State University and the University of Washington, and documentary photography and visual journalism at the International Center of Photography. He has worked as a writer, political organizer, web designer, and is a co-curator of the art series Salad, which premiered in Brooklyn in 2023. His photography focuses on spaces and the ways people create, inhabit, and navigate them.
