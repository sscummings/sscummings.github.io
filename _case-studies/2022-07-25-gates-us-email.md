### Design Within Constraints

HTML email development operates under severe technical limitations, requiring compatibility across outdated and inconsistent email clients from Microsoft Outlook to Gmail to Proton Mail and more. This project operated within Salesforce’s Pardot editor, adding an additional layer of complexity given the limits to how templates can be structured while maintaining user-friendly editing.

As the designer on the project, I approached it as a systems design challenge, balancing visual ambition with the realities of table-based layouts, variable CSS support, and workarounds for pitfalls like dark mode rendering. The goal was not perfection, but consistency and reliability across environments.

### From “Wall of Text” to Modular, Brand-Aligned Storytelling

The Gates U.S. team wanted more flexibility to move beyond dense, text-heavy emails that were difficult to read and visually stagnant &mdash; without fear of their layouts breaking on mobile screens! Through research and consultation, I drew inspiration from brands like Spotify and Starbucks to design a modular system for editors to imagine emails more like magazines, emphasizing visual hierarchy and pacing to help break content into digestible, engaging segments.

  <div class="cs-image-block image-radius-standard">
    <img src="/assets/design/2022-gatesus-03-modules.jpg" alt="Modules from Stephen Cummings's 2022 email designs for the Bill & Melinda Gates Foundation U.S. Program" loading="lazy" />
  </div>

All components were designed to align with the Gates Foundation’s updated brand guidelines and color palette while remaining practical for implementation and reuse. The system was shared across multiple newsletters as project scope expanded, with reusable code structures managed in parallel to ensure efficiency, scalability, and maintainability across templates.

</section>

### Bridging Design and Implementation

After design approval, I led the development team in building the templates from the ground up using MJML, translating design intent into robust, reusable HTML structures. I conducted extensive cross-client testing using Email on Acid alongside in-platform testing in Pardot, iterating to resolve rendering inconsistencies across supported email clients.
